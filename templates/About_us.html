Fee Management System

A fee management system is a software solution designed to streamline and automate the process of collecting and managing fees in educational institutions. It handles various functions like fee collection, payment tracking, receipt generation, and financial reporting in one platform. By digitizing these processes, fee management systems eliminate inefficiencies associated with manual methods, enhance transparency, and improve security.

Key Features:

Online Fee Payment & Receipt Generation
Student-wise Fee Tracking
Late Fee Calculation
Fee Structure Management
Admin and User Login Panels
SMS/Email Alerts for Payment Reminders
Auto-generated Reports (Daily, Monthly, Yearly)
